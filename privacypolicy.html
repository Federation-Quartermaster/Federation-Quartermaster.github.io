Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

1. Information We Collect

This application is a static, client-side tool. We do not maintain any backend databases or servers that collect, store, or process your personal data. When you connect your Roblox account, the authorization tokens (Access Token and Refresh Token) are stored securely and locally in your browser's localStorage.

2. How We Use Your Information

The authorization tokens are used exclusively to communicate directly with the Roblox Open Cloud API on your behalf. Specifically, they are used to upload the generated uniform decal directly to your Roblox inventory.

3. Information Sharing

Because we do not store your data on our servers, we do not share, sell, or rent any of your information to third parties. All API requests are made directly between your browser and Roblox's servers.

4. Third-Party Services

This tool utilizes the Roblox Open Cloud API. By using this tool to upload assets to Roblox, you are also subject to the Roblox Privacy Policy.

5. Data Deletion

You can revoke access and delete all locally stored data at any time by clicking "Disconnect from Roblox" within the application, or by clearing your browser's local storage.
